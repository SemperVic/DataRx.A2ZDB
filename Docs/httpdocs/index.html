A2ZDB Data Modelor's Guide

The purpose of this guide is to familiarize you with the various taxonomies in the A2ZDB data layer.

As more models are added and additional documentation is procurred, this guide will be revised accordingly.

Document Revision 1.1, March 2018

Getting Started

This data modelor's guide is divided into three core sections with a forth section dedicated to familiarizing yourself A2ZDB modelling principles. Regardless of your level of expertise, you are encouraged to review the A2ZDB Object Modeling Overview to get a feel for the modeling conventions A2Z uses. While the topic of data modeling can certainly be dry, It is imperitive you review this guide if you are preparing to interface with A2ZDB or perhaps even wish to extend it with your models.
